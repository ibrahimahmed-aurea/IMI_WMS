The identity of the Ship-to Node. In this case the Node is the drop location in the Route (To Node).

EarliestArrivalTimeN, CNullableDATE Earliest Arrival Time
The earliest date and time when the vehicle may arrive at the Node.

The latest date and time when the vehicle may arrive at the Node.
