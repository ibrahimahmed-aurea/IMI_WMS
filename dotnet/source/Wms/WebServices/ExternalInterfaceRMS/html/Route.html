CheckProductTransportTypeN, CstringVARCHAR2(1) Check Transport Condition
Indicates (1=Yes,0=No) if the Transport Condition of the Load must be checked
against the Transport Condition of the Departure. This attribute is inherited from Route.
If this is the case then the Transport Condition of the Load must correspond
to a Transport Condition in the Departure.
Otherwise any Transport Condition of the Load can be placed in the Departure.
