FIFOAllowedDiscrepancy N, C n4 - States the maximum number of days that the FIFO date may differ among the picked item loads. Shipping from the warehouse normally strictly occurs according to FIFO (First In First Out), but this can lead to extra long movements to drive around and pick-up the oldest item loads. To avoid this, the FIFO date for picked item loads may differ from the oldest FIFO date. Examples:
If FIFOPLUS = 0 then picking occurs strictly according to FIFO.
If FIFOPLUS = 20 then FIFO can differ with 20 days from oldest day on Item Load.
